About

Information about the Creator and Website

Our task was to create a museum exhibit for the temporary St Jogn Paul II College History Museum. Our exhibit could take form as a model, artistic work, diorama, video or more. We also had to write a 400 word explanation rationale to justify our exhibit. I (Michael Woodman) decided to make a website from scratch explaining the equipment that was apart of an Australian Soldiers Uniform.
