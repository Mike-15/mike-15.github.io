Laos - Asia

Country Information

The Lao People's Democratic Republic or Laos is a 3rd world country located Southeast Asia. In 2017 the population was recorded at 6,858,160 people which was a 1.48% increase from the 2016 population (6,758,353). It is predicted that by 2050 the population will have increased by 6.41% to 9,162,892 people, in turn creating an average of 40 people per 1km2 (worldometers, 2018). Laos has a total land area of 237,955km2 and nearly all this land mass is made up of the tropical forest biome.
